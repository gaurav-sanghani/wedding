Travel

The wedding will take place at the beautiful Horseshoe Bay Resort. They have a very good website detailing how to get to the resort.

Below is information on how to drive or fly to the Horseshoe Bay Resort and how to get around when you're here

Information

Driving

The address is:

200 Hi Circle North
Horseshoe Bay, TX 78657

Approximate drive times from nearby cities:

  • Austin: 1 hour
  • San Antonio: 1.5 hours
  • Dallas: 4 hours
  • Houston: 4 hours

Flying

The closet airports to Horseshoe Bay Resort are
  • Austin Bergstrom International (about 1 hour drive)
    • Flights to Austin
  • San Antonio International Airport (about 1.5 hour drive)
    • Flights to San Antonio

Once you have landed, it's still a fair distance to the resort. There are a few different ways you can get to the venue.

  • Driving: Please take a look at the above section on driving. Each airport offers the standard national rental car options and a few local ones.
    • Silvercar.com: They rent only Audi A4s and have convenient mobile and web app to manage your reservation. Use the promo code SEDYBPQM to get $25 off your first rental.
    • Hertz
    • Enterprise
    • Avis
    • Budget
    • Alamo
    • And many more options
  • Carpooling: If you're willing to carpool, please email your flight itinerary to info@gauravlovesradhika.com. We will attempt to match you with other guests to carpool with.
  • Shuttles: The resort offers a round-trip shuttle from the Austin airport for $390. If you would like to arrange this, please call 830-598-7946 and mention the Achari/Sanghani wedding.
  • Ride-sharing and taxicabs: Keep in mind the distance involved (60+ miles), and that some of these services may not be available in both the city of Horseshoe Bay and Austin. The following companies operate around Austin:
    • Lyft
      • According to the map on www.lyft.com/cities/austin-tx, as of September 10, 2017, Lyft operates in both Horseshoe Bay and Austin.
      • If this is your first time using Lyft, you can use the promo code ACHARISANGHANI towards your first ride.
    • RideAustin
    • Wingz
    • Uber
      • If this is your first time using Uber, you can use the promo code 8694d towards your first ride
